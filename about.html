Our Culinary Story

Legacy of Fine Dining

Located in Pamuru near NH-565 Govt Hospital junction, ADHIRATHA Family Restaurant is dedicated to preserving and serving the rich traditions of Indian food culture. We source premium, fresh farm ingredients and utilize age-old slow cooking techniques to craft true taste masterpieces.

Our restaurant features high-capacity, central air-conditioned cabins and customized family dining compartments so you can share great moments with privacy and premium comfort.

From royal Hyderabadi Dum Biryanis and Arabian Mandis to traditional Andhra spicy fish/prawn frys and delicious desserts, we offer a comprehensive menu to feed all taste buds.
