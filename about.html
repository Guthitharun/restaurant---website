Our Legacy

Legacy of Fine Dining

Located near Highway (NH-565) at the Government Hospital Junction in Pamuru, ADHIRATHA Family Restaurant (AC) is dedicated to preserving and serving the rich traditions of Indian food culture. We source premium, fresh farm ingredients and utilize age-old slow cooking techniques to craft true taste masterpieces.

Our restaurant features high-capacity, fully central air-conditioned cabins and customized family dining compartments so you can share great moments with privacy and premium comfort.

From royal Hyderabadi Dum Biryanis and Arabian Mandis to traditional Andhra spicy fish/prawn fry and delicious desserts — we offer a comprehensive menu to feed all taste buds.
